Classic Cheesecake

Course: Dessert

Description: A creamy and smooth dessert with a crunchy graham cracker crust. Perfect for special occasions or a sweet treat anytime.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 16 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Materials Needed

  • Springform pan
  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer or hand whisk
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Spatula
  • Oven

Instructions

  1. Set your oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter in a bowl. Press into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Blend until creamy and lump-free.
  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Place in the oven and bake for 45–50 minutes, or until the center is almost set.
  6. Turn off the oven and crack the door open. Let the cheesecake rest inside for 1 hour to prevent cracking.
  7. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips

  • Use room temperature ingredients for a smooth batter.
  • Don’t overmix to avoid incorporating air that can cause cracks.
  • Place a shallow pan of water on the lower oven rack for a moister baking environment (optional).
