Tomato Soup

Course:Appetizer

Description:is a soup with tomatoes as the primary ingredient. It can be served hot or cold, and may be made in a variety of ways

Tomato Soup

1. Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes (whole or crushed)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth (or chicken broth)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil or fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream or milk (optional)

Optional Garnish: Fresh basil, a drizzle of cream, croutons or toasted bread

2. Materials Needed

  • Saucepan or soup pot
  • Cutting board and knife
  • Wooden spoon or spatula
  • Blender or immersion blender
  • Ladle
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Serving bowls

3. Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onion and sauté for 5 minutes until translucent. Add garlic and cook 1 more minute.
  2. Add canned tomatoes, broth, sugar, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir and bring to a gentle boil.
  3. Lower heat and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat. Blend until smooth using an immersion blender or countertop blender.
  5. Stir in cream or milk (if using) and adjust seasoning. Reheat gently if needed.
  6. Serve hot with optional garnish: fresh basil, cream swirl, and croutons.
