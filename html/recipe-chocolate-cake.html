Chocolate Cake

Course: Dessert

Description: A rich and moist chocolate dessert perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk (or buttermilk for extra moisture)
  • ½ cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water or brewed coffee (to enhance chocolate flavor)

Materials Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • Whisk or electric mixer
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Two 9-inch round cake pans
  • Rubber spatula
  • Cooling rack
  • Oven (preheated to 350°F / 175°C)
  • Non-stick spray or butter and flour for greasing pans

Instructions

  1. Set to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans or line with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil (or butter), and vanilla. Mix until combined.
  4. tir in hot water or coffee slowly. The batter will be thin—this is normal.
  5. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate or buttercream frosting once cooled.

Tips

  • Use room temperature eggs and milk for better mixing.
  • Don’t overmix the batter—just until everything is incorporated.
  • Using coffee instead of water deepens the chocolate flavor without making it taste like coffee.
