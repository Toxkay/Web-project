Fresh Salad

Course:Appetizer

Description:This green salad recipe is simple, but it's still packed with delicious textures and flavors. It's a perfect side dish - you can serve it with almost anything!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 avocado, diced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese or shredded mozzarella (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Materials Needed

  • Cutting board and knife
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Salad tongs or two large spoons
  • Measuring spoons
  • Small bowl or jar (for dressing)

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice all the vegetables as listed and place them in a large mixing bowl.
  3. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice or vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine everything well.
  5. Add avocado and cheese (if using) just before serving to avoid sogginess.
  6. Serve fresh as a light appetizer or side dish.
