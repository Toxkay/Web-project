Lasagna

Course: Main Course

Description: Layered pasta with meat sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce (or marinara)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: fresh basil, oregano, or parsley for garnish

Materials Needed

  • Large pot (for boiling noodles)
  • Skillet or frying pan (for cooking beef)
  • Medium bowl (for mixing cheeses)
  • 9x13 inch baking dish
  • Wooden spoon or spatula
  • Aluminum foil
  • Oven (preheated to 375°F / 190°C)
  • Knife & cutting board

Instructions

  1. If using regular noodles, boil them in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside. If using no-boil noodles, skip this step.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté diced onions and garlic until translucent. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed. Stir in tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with half the Parmesan. Optionally, mix in a pinch of salt and chopped herbs.
  4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of the baking dish. Layer noodles, then ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, then a sprinkle of mozzarella. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and remaining Parmesan on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and slightly golden.
  6. Let lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing to help set the layers. Garnish with chopped parsley or basil if desired.
